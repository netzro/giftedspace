Forex Trading Notes: Mastering Pips, Leverage, and Risk

Detailed notes on forex trading essentials, covering pips, leverage, lot sizes, risk management, and market analysis strategies.
May 25, 2025 · 4 min

Forex Trading Notes: Mastering the Market#

As a Nigerian hustler diving into forex, these notes are my roadmap to understanding the game. From pips to position sizing, here’s the breakdown to trade smart and stack pips like a Lagos boss.

Pips: The Pulse of Price Movement#

  • Pip: Percentage in point, the smallest price move in forex. It’s the 4th decimal place for most pairs (e.g., EUR/USD: 1.1050 to 1.1051 = 1 pip), but 2nd decimal for JPY pairs (e.g., USD/JPY: 145.20 to 145.21 = 1 pip).
  • Pipette: 1/10 of a pip, the 5th decimal for most pairs (e.g., 1.10501), 3rd for JPY (e.g., 145.201).
  • Why it matters: Pips measure your profit or loss. Knowing pip placement is key to calculating gains in the fast-moving forex market.

Leverage: Amplify Your Hustle#

  • Leverage lets you control big trades with small capital. With $10 and 1:100 leverage, you trade $1,000.
  • Example: A $100 account with 1:500 leverage controls $50,000. A 10-pip move on a mini lot could double your money—or wipe it out.
  • Naija Tip: Leverage is like borrowing fuel for your hustle. Use it wisely, or it’ll burn you. Always pair it with solid risk management.

Lot Sizes: Scale Your Trades#

  • Standard Lot: 100,000 units = $10/pip (e.g., EUR/USD).
  • Mini Lot: 10,000 units = $1/pip.
  • Micro Lot: 1,000 units = $0.10/pip.
  • Nano Lot: 100 units = $0.01/pip.
  • Fractional Lots: You can trade 1.3 standard lots or 2.5 micro lots for flexibility.
  • Pip Value Calculation: Depends on lot size and quote currency.
    Example: EUR/USD, 2.3 standard lots:
    • 2 standard lots (2 × $10) + 3 mini lots (3 × $1) = $23/pip.
    • Or: 23 mini lots = 23 × $1 = $23/pip.
  • Naija Hustle: Start with micro lots to learn the ropes without risking your rent money.

Risk Management: Protect Your Capital#

  • Goal: Survive losing streaks without blowing your account.
  • Position Sizing: Risk only 1% of your capital per trade.
    Example: $1,000 account = $10 risk per trade. If your stop-loss is 20 pips, trade a lot size where 20 pips = $10 (e.g., 1 mini lot for EUR/USD).
  • Stop-Loss: Set a stop-loss on every trade to limit losses.
    Naija Wisdom: “No let greed scatter your plan.” Protect your capital like it’s your last plate of jollof.

Market Structure: Know the Game#

  • Analyze the market to decide: buy, sell, or stay out.
  • Trends: Is the market bullish (up), bearish (down), or ranging (sideways)?
  • Key Levels: Identify support (price floor) and resistance (price ceiling) zones.
  • Naija Analogy: Think of the market like Lagos traffic—know when to move, when to wait, or when to take another route.

Area of Value: Where to Strike#

  • Find high-probability buy/sell zones using:
    • Support/Resistance (SR): Price levels where the market often reverses or pauses.
    • Moving Averages (MA): Smooth out price action to spot trends (e.g., 50-day MA for long-term, 20-day MA for short-term).
  • Example: Buy at support with a bullish MA crossover; sell at resistance with a bearish crossover.
  • Tip: Combine SR and MA for stronger signals, like waiting for a buyer at Computer Village.

Entry Trigger: Timing Your Move#

  • Use candlestick patterns to time entries:
    • Bullish Patterns: Hammer, engulfing, morning star—signal potential buys.
    • Bearish Patterns: Shooting star, evening star—signal potential sells.
  • Example: At a support level with a bullish engulfing candle, enter a buy trade with a stop-loss below support.
  • Naija Mindset: Trade like you’re bargaining at Balogun Market—wait for the right moment, then move with confidence.

Putting It Together#

Forex isn’t a get-rich-quick scheme—it’s a skill, like learning to drive danfo in Lagos traffic. Start small, practice daily, and keep your emotions in check. Use a demo account to test these concepts, then go live with micro lots. Track every trade in a journal to spot patterns in your wins and losses. In time, you’ll trade like a pro, stacking pips and building wealth, Naija style.

Let’s hustle smart and make those dollars rain! 💸
